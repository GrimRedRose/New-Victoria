A type of Dyson Sphere that only makes up a ring around a star instead of an entire sphere. These are [[New Victorian Government/New Victoria\|New Victoria]]'s main source of power by harvesting hydrogen or other gasses from them  rather than gathering solar power, although solar power is used to power the rings themselves.
