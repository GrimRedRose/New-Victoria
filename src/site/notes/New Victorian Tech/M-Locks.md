M-Locks or Steelkin are the AI controlled robotic machines created by [[Dingostar/DingoStar Transportation\|DingoStar Transportation]] and shortly after given legal status as citizens and rights. New Victorians treat the Steelkin as brothers and sisters and can be seen working beside them in mostly hazardous or combat intensive jobs.
