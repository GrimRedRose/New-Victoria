A small mech suit designed to move large cargo through [[New Victorian Government/New Victoria\|New Victorian]] and [[Dingostar/DingoStar\|Dingostar]] cargo vessels with limited space. They have since been modified and manufactured to server different purposes including minor military application, although research is being done on further combative roles.
