Although majority of the [[New Victorian Government/New Victoria\|New Victorian]] population is atheist, a few religions have spawned, evolved, or followed into New Victorian space. These religions have ingrained themselves into New Victorian culture and are all peaceful religions and are accepted by the general public. 

**Spiritual Cosmopsychism**

Spiritual Cosmopsychism is a "science based" religion combining Spiritualism and Cosmopsychism into a single belief. It has amassed the largest religious following in New Victorian space due to its relationship with the science it claims as evidence of its existence. Followers of Spiritual Cosmopsychism believe being made of the same elements that once formed stars connects everything in the universe together into a single consciousness experiencing itself as infinite separate pieces that will eventually return to the universe's single greater conscious. They believe this is explanation for parapsychological phenomena that has happened throughout history and link many human behaviors to its theories. 

**Unity**

Founded by Hudson D. Cally, Unity is based on an old book describing Unitarianism. Hudson founded Unity on Unitarianism's principles but focused on the Abrahamic religions creating a divide in some of the passengers aboard one of the colony ships. Unity developed at first as a system to learn about and accept each groups religions to enforce peace but sooner became a singular religion under aligned concepts in each religions scripture. The movement emphasized shared laws, history, and beliefs while focusing on Unitarianism's core belief of freedom of belief, rational thought and social justice. Unity has become a popular religion for those that would have become a follower of one of the Abrahamic religions which still exist in much smaller groups. 

**Astrum Donatorism**

Usually shortened to Donatorism, the religion of the "Donator Star" believes that powerful beings, or rather the stars themselves, "donate" their energy to the New Victorians as a trade for worship. They claim that all New Victorians, followers and non-followers receive gifts from their stars for their worship even in ignorance, citing the Solar Renewal process of [[Stellar Farming\|Stellar Farming]] as worship and [[New Victorian History/New Victorians\|Halflings]] as its gifts.
