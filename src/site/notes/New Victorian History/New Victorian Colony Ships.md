The three colony ships that held what would be the [[New Victorian History/New Victorians\|New Victorians]] and eventually [[New Victorian Government/New Victoria\|New Victoria]]. All three ships would land the same moon, later named New Victoria and would form an feudal society under several warlords. The corporation that funded, built, and controlled one of the colony ships, [[Dingostar/DingoStar Transportation\|DingoStar Transportation]], would form a government and topple the warlords, uniting the people under one banner and giving them law and order.
