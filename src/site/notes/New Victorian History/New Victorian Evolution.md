The process of the people of the [[New Victorian History/New Victorian Colony Ships\|New Victorian Colony Ships]] evolving into modern [[New Victorian History/New Victorians\|New Victorians]]. 

The area of space [[New Victorian Government/New Victoria\|New Victoria]] now calls its territory is home to many dense planets with stronger gravity then most systems. The flora and fauna of these planets would evolve to endure these conditions and New Victorians would evolve to endure the flora, fauna, and gravitational conditions making them naturally strong and resilient people.
