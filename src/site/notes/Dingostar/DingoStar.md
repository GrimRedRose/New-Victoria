DingoStar was the semi-state making up the four systems under control of [[Dingostar/DingoStar Transportation\|DingoStar Transportation]]. These four systems were granted after the formation and separation of DingoStar and the [[New Victorian Government/New Victoria\|New Victorian Government]]. Ownership of these planets and connection to New Victoria granted them statehood in the new government. 

Dingostar is near fully separate from New Victoria after the formation of the [[New Victorian Parliament\|New Victorian Parliament]].
