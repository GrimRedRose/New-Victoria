The oldest corporation in [[New Victorian Government/New Victoria\|New Victoria]], tracing its origins to long before even the nation began. The corporation is credited to starting New Victoria and its government and holds some political power.

DingoStar is a massive trans galactic cargo transport company and provides New Victoria and a portion of the galaxy with its interstellar shipping. 

DingoStar is host to its own military with three branches. [[Dingostar/DingoStar Asset Recovery Forces\|DingoStar Asset Recovery Forces]] act as DingoStar's elite operators. [[Dingostar/DingoStar Security Forces\|DingoStar Security Forces]] act as security for DingoStar facilities and ships. [[Dingostar/DingoStar Naval Forces\|DingoStar Naval Forces]] are DingoStar's own navy and are mostly used for anti-piracy actions. 

DingoStar are also the ones that created the [[New Victorian Tech/M-Locks\|M-Locks]] or Steelkin. Originally created to work in hazardous environments, the Steelkin gained rights after a short deliberation over AI consciousness. Steelkin are still created for use in hazardous conditions but are able to choose their role and career, a lot of the time taking the job they were created for.
