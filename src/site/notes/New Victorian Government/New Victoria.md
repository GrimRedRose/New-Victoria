The nation consisting of [[New Victorian History/New Victorians\|New Victorians]] and the [[New Victorian Government/Victorian Sector States\|Victorian Sector States]]. New Victoria is a Federation of states branching across its territory and runs a federal government ruled by its sector states with an elected [[Chief of Federal Affairs\|Chief of Federal Affairs]]. 

New Victoria was created to release national power from [[Dingostar/DingoStar Transportation\|DingoStar Transportation]], a corporation that owned one of the three [[New Victorian History/New Victorian Colony Ships\|New Victorian Colony Ships]]. Shortly after, the newly formed government built exploration fleets and started its expansion of territory and shortly after reformed itself into a federation to distribute power. 

New Victoria has mostly atheists but hosts a few [[New Victorian History/New Victorian Religions\|religions]].
